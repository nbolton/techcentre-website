TechCentre Charity Events

Last summer, the TechCentre staff took part in a number of fundraising events. Firstly, the Royal Agricultural shows where TechCentre raised money through a Mouse throwing competition.

The competition was won by Abbie Williams who took away a new laptop.

The staff also undertook a sponsored weight loss, in which the team collectively lost weight. After this successful campaign and the success of the Royal Shows we managed to raise £1200. The money was split equally between our two chosen charities, Manx Cancer Help and Robert Owen House. This was donated at our Client Christmas Party which took place in Bar George.

This summer we will once again be taking part in the Royal Agricultural Show on 13th and 14th August. The ever popular Mouse throwing competition will be making a comeback along with other fun filled games and activities.

This year we will be supporting a very worthy cause, the Friends of Chernobyl’s Children. The charity's aim is to relieve the suffering of children affected by the radiation explosion from the nuclear plant at Chernobyl.

To find out more please visit the website www.focc.co.im

All the staff at TechCentre would like to extend their thanks to everyone who took part in and supported our fundraising efforts. Without your support we would not have been able to achieve such a grand total. We hope that you will continue to support us throughout the upcoming year as we attempt to raise money for charity!
